Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It wlil become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oli, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yuellow onions, thinkly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oli, for crushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a mediuim-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the four is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the slough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour..
